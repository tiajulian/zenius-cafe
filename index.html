Our Story

Where every
cup is a canvas

Nestled on Abercrombie Street in Sydney's vibrant Chippendale neighbourhood, Zenius Coffee is a sanctuary for those who believe great coffee is an art form.

Our baristas craft each cup with precision and passion — from velvety flat whites to intricate latte art featuring seahorses, bees, bunnies and beyond. Using premium Deluca beans, every pour tells a story.

Paired with a thoughtfully curated brunch menu, we invite you to slow down, savour the moment, and experience coffee the way it was always meant to be.

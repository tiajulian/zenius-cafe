Our Story

A corner of
Chippendale we call home

Big windows, warm bricks, the smell of fresh coffee and a community that feels like family. That's Zenius, your favourite morning stop on Abercrombie Street.

Our baristas pour with purpose, crafting everything from silky flat whites to incredible latte art featuring seahorses, bunnies, bees and beyond, using premium Deluca beans.

Pull up a seat out front, bring your dog, order the pandan hotcakes, and stay a while. This is what a good morning looks like.
